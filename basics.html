Stoicism is a philosophy that concentrates on controlling your mind and emotions, and enjoy the present moment as it is, all to ultimately reach happiness.

The cardinal virtues

Wisdom
Courage
Justice
Temperance

The principles

Don't get affected by external events that you can't control. You choose your reactions.
Act in accordance to reason and nature.
You have a duty to think of your society's good.
Always remember and reflect abouth Death, it's inevitability and closeness.
Think with introspection. Think about yourself from a different, bigger point of view : that of the universe, time and eternity.
